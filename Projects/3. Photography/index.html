About Founder

Alec Soth is an American photographer based on Minneapolis. Soth makes "large-scale American projects" featuring the midwestern US. NY Times art critic Hilary M. Sheets wrote that he has made a "photographic career out of finding chemistry with strangers" and photographs "loners and dreamers".
